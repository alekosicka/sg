Our voice and positioning

The Mission

Justpoint’s mission is to bring transparency and fairness to the consumer protection and legal industries. We use data to help people understand their rights and access justice.

The Two Entities

Justpoint Inc.

The research and technology arm. It is scientific, data-driven, and objective. It identifies risks and trends.

Justpoint PLLC

The law firm. It is protective, authoritative, and client-focused. It acts on the findings to provide legal representation.
